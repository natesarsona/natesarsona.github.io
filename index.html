rambling thoughts 01/06/2023

well happy new year. let's kick things into gear

it's evident i've put this site off into my peripherals simply because all of december is one long period that everyone collectively agrees to not put pressure on deadlines.

not that i have any.

upon the annual tradition of self-reflection (that usually only occurs for the first three weeks of the new year) i've come to realize that I am blessed with the drive to maintain resourcefulness on the internet.

i probably can't go into detail about what exactly i needed to scavenge from the depths of private forums and gated online communities, however, i will say i am now fully equipped to learn industry standard practices in computer graphics and 3d modelling.

also, i gifted myself a graphics tablet finally.. long overdue, right?

anyways, time to go back to sculpting and then updating the portfolio. but here's my monthly check-in to whomever this site may concern

thanks for making it this far. check out my uploads and send me a dm. my socials are accessible somewhere on here
